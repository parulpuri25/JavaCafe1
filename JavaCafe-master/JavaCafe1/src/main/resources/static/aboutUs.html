Our History

Brewing Excellence Since 1995 Java Cafe is a renowned coffee shop that has been serving exceptional coffee since its establishment in 1995. What started as a small corner cafe has grown into a beloved destination for coffee enthusiasts and a hub for the local community.

The founder, Parul Puri, had a deep passion for coffee and a dream to create a space where people could gather, relax, and savor the finest coffee blends. With her unwavering commitment to quality and a genuine love for the craft, Parul set out to create a coffee shop experience like no other. In the early days, Java Cafe operated out of a modest space with just a few tables and a small roasting area. But word quickly spread about the rich, aromatic coffees being brewed at Java Cafe, and the demand grew exponentially. As Java Cafe gained popularity, it expanded its menu to offer an extensive selection of specialty coffees sourced from the finest coffee-growing regions around the world. From bold and robust espresso shots to delicate pour-overs and creamy lattes, there was a perfect cup of coffee to suit every taste.

In 2005, Java Cafe opened its second location in a bustling downtown district, further establishing its presence as a premier coffee destination. Today, with multiple locations and a dedicated team of passionate baristas, Java Cafe continues to be a beacon of coffee excellence. The coffee shop has become a cherished gathering spot for friends, families, and professionals alike.
